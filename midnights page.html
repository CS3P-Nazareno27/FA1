Midnights

Meet me at midnights

Midnights is the tenth studio album by
American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift, released on October
21, 2022, via Republic Records. Announced at the 2022 MTV Video
Music Awards, Midnights marked Swift's first new body of work since her
2020 indie folk albums, Folklore and Evermore. It is a moody concept album
about nocturnal ruminations, written and produced by Swift with longtime collaborator Jack Antonoff.(Wikipedia)
